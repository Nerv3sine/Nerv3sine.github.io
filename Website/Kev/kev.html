Route 1

Route Link
  • Try to keep within [40-50] preferably [45-50], going over speed limit doesn’t mean penalization but try to keep goin over to a minimum
  • If you’re crossing a sidewalk, that acts as a stop sign! make sure to stop before it, then proceed to do anything else
  • may have people driving below speed limit on the freeway, change lanes (if there’s lots of space to reach up to speed limit, no required to unless the examiner tells u otherwise)
  • be careful of the stop sign at the top of the hill, people often fail here
  • making mistakes like not turning when they ask u to is fine, just make sure you’re making safe decisions rather than going according to the route you’re supposed to take
  • route had no pullovers or anything, simply drive through the route

Route 2

Route Link
  • [On a right turn situation] You’re not obligated to turn right at an intersection with a red light if there’s a car on your left side blocking your visibility → wait for the light to turn before right turning
  • be careful of the 4 way stop sign immediately after the right turn
  • Beware of the 50 speed limit sign hidden in the greenery on the freeway exit to Hastings (sign located in halfway in between the freeway and Hastings St)
  • When turning onto a road with a designated bus lane, turning onto the bus lane is fine as long as you change off soon afterwards. (I just merged right onto the road for the one on Lougheed highway)
  • parallel park, pullover, back-in park, name 5 hazards were done/mentioned
  • checking mirrors frequently was mentioned
